About

Two crafts, one purpose

MC Office is a sole proprietorship based in Japan, built on two seemingly opposite strengths: the logic of business automation and the artistry of the violin.

From streamlining everyday workflows with Google Apps Script to performing at weddings and cafés, the goal never changes — supporting people in whatever form serves them best, whether that's a tool or a tune.
